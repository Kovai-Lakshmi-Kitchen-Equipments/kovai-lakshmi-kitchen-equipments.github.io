LADOO MAKING MACHINE

Capacity: 700-800 Output/Hr
Motor: 1/2hp Motor With Gear Box For Roller, 1hp Motor With Gear Box Extruder
Body: Fully Stainless Steel 304 Grade
Function: Fully Controlled By Sensor And Relay, Variable Speed Can Be Achieved With 1hp Compressor

Our Ladoo Making Machine is designed to simplify and speed up the traditional ladoo preparation process. Built with high-quality stainless steel and advanced food-grade components, it ensures hygiene, uniformity, and high productivity. This machine is ideal for sweet shops, catering units, and large-scale food production industries looking to automate their ladoo making.

Available in various capacities, our machine can efficiently prepare different types of ladoos such as Boondi Ladoo, Besan Ladoo, Rava Ladoo, Coconut Ladoo, and more.

How to Use

  1. Prepare the mixture: Make your ladoo mixture (boondi, besan, coconut, etc.) ready as per your recipe.
  2. Load the machine: Place the prepared mixture into the hopper of the ladoo making machine.
  3. Adjust settings: Set the desired ladoo size and shape using the control panel.
  4. Start the machine: Switch on the machine; it will automatically roll and shape the ladoos uniformly.
  5. Collect the ladoos: The ready ladoos will be discharged from the outlet, perfectly round and consistent in size.
Note: The machine is easy to clean and maintain after each use.

Benefits of Buying a Ladoo Making Machine

  • Increases Productivity: Produces large quantities of ladoos in a short time with minimal manpower.
  • Uniform Shape & Size: Ensures professional-quality ladoos every time.
  • Hygienic Operation: Made with food-grade stainless steel for clean and safe preparation.
  • Reduces Labor Costs: Minimizes the need for manual rolling, saving time and effort.
  • Easy to Operate & Maintain: User-friendly design with simple controls and easy cleaning.
  • Versatile Use: Suitable for various ladoo types and sweet preparation.
